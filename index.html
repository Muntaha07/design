Our Web Design Process

  • Consultation

    We discuss with you and learn about your business and set goals for your project

  • Research

    We study more about your industry and competitors to ensure you get the best visual solution possible

  • Sketch

    We draw some ideas and come up with initial plans.

  • Concepts

    We settle on some initial design drafts for your website.

  • Present

    We show these concepts and drafts to you ,explaining the rationale behind it and why they will be successfull.

  • Revise

    We ask you to choose one concept and then we continue to revise it till all your goals are met and you are happy.

  • Launch

    We upload the final approved design on the internet for everybody to appreciate

  • Deliver

    When we are all good to go,we deliver you the final website for your approval.
